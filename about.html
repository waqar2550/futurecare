About us

Our mission| vision & plans

In a country like ours, where we are facing issues like poverty, unemployment, poor health and illiteracy are important contributing factors in our awareness of the economic and social injustices among the people. These factors have negative influence on the quality of life of our people. Mostly the vulnerable are the greatly affected sectors of our society, the women and children, not mentioning the unemployed and disadvantaged. In these seeming hopeless and helpless situations, our organization is hoping to gather our collective humanitarian efforts to offer HOPE to the community our compassionate services. It is our dream to have a community where everyone can live in dignity and quality of life. It is our mission to reach out to those vulnerable and disadvantaged and let them feel cared, protected, wanted and loved. Concretely we focus our effort in offering to vulnerable and poor children quality education and providing opportunities for women to claim their rightful place in the society by providing them chance to develop their skills and help provide income to their family. We also feel the suffering of those who are sick, that’s why we are also providing medical camp in different depressed areas in Pakistan. So in the spirit of human solidarity, let us gather together our energy and strength to share responsibility in caring and bringing HOPE to our most needy brothers and sisters. We hope to live in reverence to life and humble enough to give thanks to the Giver of Life and offer our services with joy and trust with each other’s generosity and commitment. “As long as there are people who are suffering, our organization continues to offer HOPE for them.”
